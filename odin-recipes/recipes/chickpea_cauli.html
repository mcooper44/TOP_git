Chickpea Cauliflower Curry

Description

This one gets better in the fridge and makes great leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Can o' Chickpeas
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Grated Ginger
  • Diced Tomato
  • Grated Carrot
  • Head of Cauliflower
  • Garam Masala
  • Tumeric
  • Parika
  • Pepper or chiles
  • Salt
  • Chopped coriander leaves and stems

Instructions

  1. Break apart the cauliflower and chop the stem and leaves.
  2. Cook the onion, garlic and ginger in a pan with some oil until fragrant.
  3. Add Carrots and continue cooking for a few minutes then add the cauliflower, adding more oil as needed.
  4. Add chicpeas and mix thoroughly adding the diced tomato and half of the spices. As the chickpeas begin to cook mash some with a spoon to thicken the liquid as it reduces. Then add the coriander. When almost finished cooking add the remaining spices.
  5. Let sit uncovered for 5 minutes and then serve.
