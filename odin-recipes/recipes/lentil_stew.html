Red Lentil Stew

Description

This is a standard but delicious staple of the weekly dinner rotation. It is an easy one to make and quick to clean up

Ingredients

  • Half or 3/4 cup red lentils (thoroughly washed)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Grated Ginger
  • Diced Tomato
  • Grated Carrot
  • Garam Masala
  • Tumeric
  • Salt
  • Lime Juice

Instructions

  1. Cook the Lentils until soft and then drain and set asside
  2. Cook the onion, garlic and ginger in a pan with some oil until fragrant
  3. Add Carrots and continue cooking for a few minutes then add the diced tomato and the spices.
  4. Add lentils and mix thoroughly - simmer until ready and add lime juice and garnish with lime wedges and chopped coriander leaves to taste.
