Udon Noodle Soup

Description

This recipe made it's way into the dinner rotation during this last winter. The broth is key.

Ingredients

  • Shitake and other mushrooms
  • Spring Onion
  • Garlic
  • Grated Ginger
  • Sliced Carrot
  • Eggplant
  • Greens
  • Tofu
  • Udon noodles
  • Broth - in a pinch Pho Broth is easy to get
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Instructions

  1. Cook mushrooms and vegetables in a pot with oil with garlic and ginger. When ready add the tofu and simmer until ready to add the broth.
  2. Add the broth and any additional seasoning to taste. Add noodles and simmer briefly before serving.
